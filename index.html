Welcome to the Aeroclub Novy Svet

About us

Aeroclub Novy Svet is a society of people joined together by their common hobby - flying. The club is situated at a small sport airport in Novy Svet where it allowes all interested people to meet and train themselves in their hobby: motor flying, gliding or parachuting.
