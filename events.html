Pribina Cup 2019

As every year there is again a gliding competition Pribina Cup 19.-27. April 2019 in Nitra. The CoR recommends that its pilots pay particular attention to flights in and around Nitra Airport and practically throughout Slovakia. More than ever, contact with Bratislava Info on frequency 124.3 is important for flights. Frequency Nitra operation: 123,405. 145 pilots take part in the competition, so airspace will be busy. It is necessary to count with the landing of gliders at the surrounding airports.

Novy Svet Air Rally 2019

The chairman of the LKS informs the club members and all flying fans that on June 8, 2019 an Air Rally flight competition will take place at Novy Svet airport. Snacks for all will be provided. In case of bad weather, the flight is shifted by one week, ie. 06/15/2019

Light Up The Sky

Aeroclub Novy Svet announces year-round inter-club gliding competition to use the weather at the beginning and end of the season, for beginners as well as for pilots who do not like to move away from the airport and want to compete. the triangle is built out of confined spaces so that it can fly at any time in good weather. So polish the elbows and log flights. The duration of the competition is from 14 February to 15 October.
