Minimal Requirements for PPL (A)/SEP training:

Age

The minimum age for enrollment is 15 years(in case of minors parent consent is required.

The minimum age for obtaining a PPL (A) licence is 17 years.

Age for the first independent flight

The minimum age for making the first solo flight is 16 years.

Medical fitness requirements

Before the first single flight, the applicant must have a medical fitness of min. 2nd class.

Radiotelephony card

Holder of a radiotelephony card issued by the Regulatory Office for Electronic Communications and Postal Services(for more information on obtaining a licence)
